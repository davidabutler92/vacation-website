My dream vacation

I would love to visit the Bahamas. It has been one of my most desired destinations since I was a child.

Below is a list of things I would love to do in the Bahamas.

  • Snorkeling through the coral reefs
  • Beach side lounging with a margarita
  • Surfing those huges swells!
  • Spear fishing for some monster groupers
  • Swimming with some big sharks
  • Touring through Nassau and Freeport, tasting all the native cuisine
